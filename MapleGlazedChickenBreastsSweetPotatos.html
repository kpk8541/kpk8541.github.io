Maple-Glazed Skillet Chicken Breasts with Sweet Potato Hash

A Kyle contribution

Chicken
2-4 boneless skinless chicken breast
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup white whole wheat flour
2 tablespoons olive oil

Vegetables
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 medium sweet potatoes peeled and diced into 1/2-in cubes
1 small onion
2 cloves garlic
1 teaspoon (smoked) paprika
Salt/pepper to taste

Maple Glaze
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 clove garlic minced
3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup maple syrup
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 400F
Prep the chicken. Pat chicken dry and season with salt and pepper on both sides. Dredge chicken breasts in flour. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil until shimmery. Place chicken in a pan and brown on both sides, 3-4 minutes on each side until golden-brown. Transfer chicken to a plate and set aside.
Saute the vegetables. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in the same skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Saute sweet potatoes, onion, and garlic in oil 5-8 minutes until veggies are golden and caramelized (sweet potatoes wonâ€™t be cooked through) Stir in paprika and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Put everything in the oven. Arrange chicken breasts around the sweet potatoes in a skillet. Bake at 400F 20-25 minute
Make the sauce. In a small skillet over medium-high heat, heat olive oil then saute garlic in oil until just fragrant. Stir in vinegar, syrup, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring sauce to a boil, then reduce heat to medium and let simmer until reduced and thickened, about 10 minutes.
Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables. When the chicken and veggies are done in the oven, pour the sauce over everything and serve.
